~About us~

More about us

Scroll to find out more about our business!

Using only the best ingredients and flours from France and Japan, Himbo Bakeries delivers scrumptious Viennoiseries and freshly baked artisan breads. Foodies from all over the world have praised Himbo's baked goods, including our sourdough, flaky buttery crossaint, kouign-amann, instagram-famous cruffins, and Basque Burnt Cheesecake, among other items. We are your one-stop deli shop in the heartland. Knowing that the ingredients we utilize are always of the highest caliber, ensures your enjoyment with every bite.

In its capacity as a neighborhood bakery, Himbo is dedicated to social responsibility, supporting group community projects, environmental initiatives, people with mental health issues, student internship programs, and mission work. To spread love and cheer to families and friends, staff and clients, caregivers and volunteers, and other groups and individuals, we believe in delighting customers by creating pastry gift packs on their behalf.

We always like serving our butter halves, cheesin' and loafin' them in our little bakery. Please come by for a slice of life and share the loaf!

Our Mission & Values

At Himbo Bakeries, we strive to offer our customers products and services that are affordable, valuable, and of high quality. We are committed to developing more delectable and inventive cheesecake flavors and welcoming more customers like you to try our desserts.
